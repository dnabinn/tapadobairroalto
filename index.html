A Nossa História

Tapas with Portuguese
& Spanish Soul

Located in the heart of historic Bairro Alto, Tapa do Bairro Alto is a place where traditional Portuguese and Spanish tapas come together with creativity, passion, and the finest fresh ingredients.

Inspired by the flavors of the sea and authentic recipes, every dish is crafted to bring a true taste of Lisbon to your table.

In a warm and welcoming atmosphere, with both indoor and outdoor seating, every meal becomes a journey through the rich tastes of Portugal and Spain.

From classic tapas to signature creations, everything is prepared with care, quality, and respect for tradition.
